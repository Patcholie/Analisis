HELP AND SUPPORT


Helpful Tips:

  • Try to write informative prompts, describe how you got the injury, how does the injury feel (itching, hot, etc.) and such, though don't write too much.
  • Try to center the injury in the middle of the camera (The + in the middle of the live camera view) for best results.
  • Try to be as accurate as you can when it comes to the pain level (The description of the pain level does not need to exactly describe how painful the injury is).
  • The selections under the 'ANALISIS' button should describe how much available resources you have at your place (It does not have to be accurate).
  • The body part selection is meant to help the output be more accurate, try to select the area that fits the best.
  • Soon we will add more features and make ANALISIS more accurate and overall better (Including UI Changes, Model Changes, etc.).

Examples:

Ex: An Example of a good prompt:
- A mosquito bit me, it itches and it happened a few minutes ago.
Ex: An Example of good pain level according to this prompt:
- Mild pain (discomforting but can be ignored).
Ex: An Example of a good prompt:
- I got shot by a gun, I am bleeding and I can't control it.
Ex: An Example of good pain level according to this prompt:
- Worst pain imaginable, paramedics are required immediately (unbearable and may lead to unconsciousness)
